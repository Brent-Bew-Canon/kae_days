Therapist for Members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

Faith-Based, Christ-Centered Therapy

Welcome to Kae Days Therapy. I’m Kaelynn, and as a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, I offer therapy that honors your mental health needs while respecting your faith. I believe healing is most powerful when it supports the whole person, including your mind, body, and spirit. My methodology will provide support as you utilize Jesus Christ as the foundation of true change.

I understand the importance of working with a therapist who appreciates the role faith plays in your life. Whether you're facing trauma, substance abuse, personality disorders, OCD, or eating disorders, I strive to create a space where you feel safe, heard, and supported, with therapy that aligns with gospel principles and your relationship with the Savior.

Areas of Expertise

I specialize in working with individuals navigating a range of challenges. My therapy blends evidence-based practices with a gospel-centered perspective to support your healing journey.

  • Trauma Recovery
  • Substance Abuse Treatment
  • Personality Disorders (Narcissistic and Histrionic Traits)
  • Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD)
  • Eating Disorders

An Approach That Respects Your Faith

Healing is a personal process, and for those who wish to include prayer, scripture-based insights, or faith discussions in therapy, I am happy to integrate those elements. Whether or not you choose to bring spirituality into sessions, you can trust that your beliefs will be honored throughout our work together.

I believe in the Savior’s ability to heal and strengthen us. If you're looking for therapy that addresses mental health challenges while supporting your testimony and spiritual goals, I would be honored to walk alongside you.
