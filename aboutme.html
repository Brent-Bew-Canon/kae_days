About Me

EMDR Therapist Kaelynn Buchanan

Kaelynn specializes in working with those struggling with depression, anxiety, relationship issues, and trauma. She uses a trauma informed lens and is trained in Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing Therapy (EMDR), Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavioral Therapy (DBT), and mindfulness techniques.

Kaelynn believes everyone has different talents, strengths, and experiences that mold people into unique individuals. She is aware that clients are coming to her in less-than-ideal circumstances and works to create an empathetic and non-judgmental space where clients can be heard, validated, and supported.

She believes in treating the whole person and trying to see the bigger picture of the person's overall life for a comprehensive approach. Kaelynn earned her Bachelor's Degree in Family Science with an emphasis in human development and a Master's Degree in Social Work with an emphasis in clinical practice. She also has a certificate in Assessment of Integrative Health Modalities.

She has worked in residential, intensive out-patient, out-patient, and academic settings with children, adolescents, and adults.
